What is this?

This simple web app allows you to generate or regenerate a password for any of your websites or applications. Simply fill in the fields below to generate a password.

You can also reinsert the same date to find a password you regenarated before. Technically, nobody can find out about your generated passwords unless they can figure out the exact domain and master password, although I do not guarantee anything.
